The Climate Challenge We All Face

Every year, human activity releases over 50 billion tons of greenhouse gases into our atmosphere. The consequences are already here - rising temperatures, extreme weather, and ecosystem collapse.

Rising Temperatures

Global average temperature has increased by 1.1°C since pre-industrial times, with devastating effects.

Extreme Weather

More frequent and intense hurricanes, droughts, and floods threaten communities worldwide.

Biodiversity Loss

1 million species are at risk of extinction due to climate change and habitat destruction.
